# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Welcome different viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers through repository security/contact channels.
