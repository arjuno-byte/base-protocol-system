# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful,
constructive, and welcoming for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about technical concerns.
- Focus feedback on code, design, operations, and security.
- Welcome different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Help keep discussions actionable and easy to follow.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private infrastructure details
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict
participation for behavior that violates this code. Enforcement decisions should
be proportional, documented when appropriate, and focused on protecting the
community and the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers through the repository
security or contact channels.
